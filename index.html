About Me

Hi, I'm Marcelo Duarte, a talented graphic designer and filmmaker with over 3 years of experience. I'm from Cape Verde and I'm passionate about art, design and telling stories through cinema.

Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to work on a variety of creative projects, ranging from logo and visual identity design to video and film production.
I believe in the importance of transmitting a clear and impactful message through visual and audiovisual media.
I use the latest design and video editing tools and software to create unique, high-quality pieces. My goal is to exceed my clients' expectations by delivering work that communicates effectively and emotionally.

On this site you will find a selection of my best work, which demonstrates my style and skills. I invite you to explore my portfolio and delve into the visual and cinematic world I create. If you are looking for a passionate and committed graphic designer and filmmaker, I am ready to collaborate on exciting projects. Get in touch to discuss how we can work together and create something amazing.
